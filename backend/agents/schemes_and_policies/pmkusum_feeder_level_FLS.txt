**PM-KUSUM Component C – Feeder Level Solarisation (FLS)**

• CFA: ₹1.05 crore per MW in standard regions.  
• In special regions (NE, hilly states, islands): CFA: ₹1.75 crore per MW.  
  :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

• Project duration: About **25 years** (typical solarisation lifespan).  
  :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

• Enables states to solarise agricultural feeders in CAPEX or RESCO mode, reducing subsidy burden and improving daytime irrigation supply.
