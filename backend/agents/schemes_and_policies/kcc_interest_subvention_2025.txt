**Kisan Credit Card (KCC) — MISS Cap Increase & Subvention Details (Budget 2025–26)**

• **MISS loan limit raised** from ₹3 lakh to **₹5 lakh** as announced in Union Budget 2025–26.  
  :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

• **Miss Subvention & Prompt Repayment Incentive (PRI):**
   - Interest Subvention to banks: **1.5%**
   - Prompt Repayment Incentive to farmers: **up to 3%**
   → Effective interest becomes **~4%** if repaid promptly.  
  :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

• **Collateral-free loan limits:**
   - Up to **₹2 lakh** collateral-free (RBI circular Dec 2024)
   - Under select bank tie-ups, up to **₹3 lakh** may be offered without collateral.  
  :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

• Cabinet approval ensures **MISS structure continues for FY 2025–26 at 1.5% IS**, covering short-term loans to ₹3 lakh (and up to ₹5 lakh per Budget).  
  :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
