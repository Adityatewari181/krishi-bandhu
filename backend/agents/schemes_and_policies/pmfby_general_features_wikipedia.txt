**PMFBY — Scheme Overview (Wikipedia Summary)**

• Launched: 18 February 2016  
• Insured Risks:
   - Prevented Sowing/Planting/Germination  
   - Standing Crop Losses (natural fire, lightning, storm, hailstorm, cyclone, flood, drought, pests, landslide, etc.)  
   - Post-Harvest Losses up to 2 weeks for crops in cut & spread condition  
   - Localized Calamities (hailstorm, landslide, inundation, cloudburst, natural fire due to lightning)  
   - Optional add-on for wild animal attacks in certain states  
  :contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}

• Coverage: Initially compulsory for loanee farmers; voluntary since 2020 for others.
